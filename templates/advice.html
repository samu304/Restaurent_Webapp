I N G R E D I E N T's   U S E D

For the Dough:

  • 120 grams refined flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt water (for kneading)

For the Chicken Fillings:

  • 1 cup chicken (minced)
  • 1/2 cup onions (finely chopped)
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper powder
  • 1 tsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp soya sauce
  • Salt
  • 1/4 tsp vinegar

For the Vegetarian Filling:

  • 1 cup carrot and cabbage, grated
  • 2 tbsp onions, finely chopped
  • 1/2 tsp garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/4 tsp vinegar
  • 1/2 tsp soya sauce
  • to taste salt
  • to taste pepper
  • 1 tbsp cornflour

For Chilli Sauce:

  • 25 gram garlic, peeled
  • 6 grams whole red chillies
  • 3 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • to taste salt
  • to taste sugar
